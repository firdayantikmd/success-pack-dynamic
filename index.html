Our recycled LDPE and PET undergo thorough processing to meet stringent food safety regulations. They are tested and certified to ensure they are safe for direct contact with food, maintaining high standards of quality and safety.

We conduct rigorous testing and adhere to strict quality control measures to ensure the high quality of our recycled plastic products.

Yes, we offer customization services to meet specific requirements. Our team works closely with clients to deliver tailored solutions.
